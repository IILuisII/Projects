The data is relevant demonstrating relatedness to the markers being measured. Poverty and absence of healthcare are two extremes. The majority of those without healthcare are in the 35 - 42 age range. The presence of obesity and smokers do not shift by much depending on the average age range. The average age of all this data from 2014 seems to fall within that range.
